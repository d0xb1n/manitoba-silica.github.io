Welcome to Manitoba's Mining Industry

Manitoba's mining industry has a long-standing history and is a key player in the province's economy. The Mining Association of Manitoba (MAMI) advocates for the industry's interests, emphasizing the importance of Manitoba's minerals for the global green economy.
